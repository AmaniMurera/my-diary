my summer adventure

This summer All things I did this summer are very interesting.I have the opportunity to visited Togo. This was very wonderfull, I did not have much to do other than enjoy and dance classic music. It was the funniest and amazing moment.

Another great thing that really stood out in my summer was that I met new people from other countries of the world including Wisconsin and the UK and Germany. First I ran away to England. This is the most exciting experience I have ever experienced. this summer not like my other summers I had some new experiences and memories that make me feel good and smile. as this summer is very hot I spend much time in the swimming pool.

In addition,I have got many jobs one of them was working as system administarter in Kigali institute of technology. then I would work as IT supporter in the Kigali commercial bank, the best thing off this were all the new peaple that I meet during my adventure. Next I had another job as networking engineer in Kigali electronic service. those were the jobs that I had over the summer and My experience thought me a tons of things about control myself and managing time.
